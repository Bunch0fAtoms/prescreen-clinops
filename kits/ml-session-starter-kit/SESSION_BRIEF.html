1 What this session is

Applied AI, not model training

There is no classifier to fit or tune. A language model does the reading. Deterministic rules make the eligibility call, with a plain-English reason for every patient.

✅ This session is
  • Applied AI feature extraction. ai_query reads notes to pull HER2, ER, and PR status.
  • Rigorous evaluation. MLflow scores the extraction against a ground-truth set.
  • A governed, auditable pre-screen with a reason per patient.
✕ This session is not
  • Training a predictive classifier.
  • Hyperparameter tuning or feature engineering a model.
  • A black-box "probability of eligibility."
2 The catch, and the whole point

Some patients live only in the notes

Biomarker status is not always in the structured tables. For about 60 of our 300 synthetic patients, HER2 / ER / PR status was only ever written into a free-text pathology note. A SQL query over measurement alone silently misses them.

5 Two things that make it real

Trials are data. Governance is visible.

Trials are data, not hardcoded rules

Eligibility criteria live in the DE group's silver_trial_criteria table. Your pre-screen joins it instead of hardcoding Trial A/B logic. A patient qualifies when each non-null requirement matches and age is in range. Because it is a join, a brand-new Trial C screens with no code change.

Governance is visible, not hidden

Models register to Unity Catalog, lineage is automatic, and the gold layer carries the biomarker_source audit column. Every eligibility decision traces back to structured data or an AI reading of a note. The Governance group's masks and filters apply to your tables and model as you build them.

The synthetic → real toggle

The whole kit runs on synthetic data by default. When Fred Hutch is ready, flip one bundle variable to point at real curated_omop.omop. The table names are identical, so every query you wrote runs unchanged.

6 The coordinator app

Where a human stays in charge

Sita asked for a researcher-facing app on top of the pre-screen. We built it as this kit's inspiration demo. One principle: the model informs, the human decides.

Timeline drill-down

Pick a patient, see their tests over time from the longitudinal gold table.

Override write-back

A coordinator can disagree and remove a patient, with a reason. The model output stays immutable. The app shows effective eligibility as human-over-model. Auditable and reversible.

In-app agent

A small agent checks a patient against all trials or screens a subset, calling the Foundation Model directly.
